Who am I ?

I'm Micaela Pimenta*, but everybody calls me Micas, I'm 22 and I'm from Braga, I'm fresh out college where I graduated in Computer Graphics and Multimedia Engineer, we done things like maths and programming but what catch me most was Design, 3D Modeling and Motion Graphics and Video Editing. So my interest area is design, I love to solve problems throw design interfaces\ icon or Logos and thinking how will it affect the user. For now I'm dedicated the most to icons, may because they are small like me.
